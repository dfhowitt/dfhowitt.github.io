About

Born in Tokyo, raised in London and educated at LSE, I have travelled and worked across the world. During my time at both HSBC and Commerzbank I developed strong analytical skills and became a finance associate at Qadre where I was responsible for financial reporting, analysis and modelling.

I also led the research team and managed relationships with prospective clients to consult on and design tech solutions for companies in the trade finance, post-trade and corporate admin industries. After a year of working closely with the talented tech team I was ambitious to upskill and learn how to become a full stack web developer at Le Wagon.

Before starting the bootcamp, I travelled coast-to-coast in Australia, through Southeast Asia and then lived in Tokyo where I studied N3 level Japanese.

At Le Wagon I led our project team in launching a live web application which stimulated my appetite for design and end-to-end project management. Moreover, I enjoy problem solving and my mathematical competency and background in finance make me a strong backend engineer. Since then I have been working on freelance and personal projects in web development and data analytics. I am open to any opportunity so feel free to message me!
