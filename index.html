About

Born in Tokyo, raised in London and educated at LSE, I have travelled and worked across the world. During my time at both HSBC and Commerzbank I was given significant responsibility which trained me as finance associate for my most recent job in the blockchain space.

At Qadre I was responsible for financial reporting, analysis and modelling. I led the research team and managed relationships with prospective clients to design tech solutions for companies in the trade finance, post-trade and corporate admin industries. After a year of working closely with the talented tech team I was ambitious to upskill and learn how to become a full stack web developer at Le Wagon.

Before starting the bootcamp, I travelled coast-to-coast in Australia, throughout Southeast Asia, and then lived in Tokyo and studied N3 level Japanese.

I enjoy problem solving and my mathematical competency and background in finance make me a strong backend engineer. At Le Wagon I led our project team in launching a live web application which stimulated my appetite for design and end-to-end project management.

Since then I have been working on freelance and personal projects. I am open to any opportunity so feel free to message me!
